AirBNB Berlin

Optimal Price Predictor


AirBNB Berlin is a web application for listing a property in Berlin for rent. After entering a street address, location, type of house, and other details, the Optimal Pricing algorithm will utilize historical booking data from Berlin to predict an optimal price to list the property at.

Meet The Team

The team behind AirBNB Optimal consists of a Data Science group, a BackEnd and FrontEnd group, and a User Experience designer.
